Helping our Earth, Saving Trees

Approved by Mother Earth: yes
Awesome Rating: 100% awesome

Are you hoping to be more involved with social activism for our environment? Give back to our trees who clean our air, remove the pollutants, and are essential to living. You don't have to go off planting hundreds of trees to make a difference, minor details in your lifestyle can impact more than you may think.

  • Things You'll Need

  • A Good Heart
  • A Love for Nature & Life
  • Respect for our Planet
  • Desire for a Better Place
  • Your Voice or Expression
  • Kindness

  1. Use less paper

    Whenever you can, reduce your amount of paper use. Paper production is one of the biggest problems for the environment. Try paperless billing, reusable coffee mugs for cafes, reusable water bottles, buying reclaimed furniture, buying used books, moving with plastic bins instead of carboard boxes, etc.

  2. Buy recycled paper products

    Read materials closely to see if it has been made from recycled materials, or even recycle yourself! Do your research to see which companies are sustainable and truthful to their word with helping the environment.

  3. Use cloth products over paper whenever possible

    Because you can wash and reuse cloth, it is a better alternative to one use paper. Reusable items are always the more sustainable route to choose! A smart tip is to bring a reusable tote to the store instead of using the plastic bags provided.

  4. Eat less meat

    Factory-raised animals require lots of space to be prepared for meat consumption. This means that the more meat being eaten, the more trees and acres of forests/woodlands needed to be cut down and ruined.

  5. Avoid products with palm oil

    Palm oil is a very problematic substance for our trees and forests. Palm oil production requires an insanse amount of land, which means they are again tearing down forests and habitats where animals live in order for one small ingredient. Check your labels to make sure you are not buying anything with palm oil!

  6. Spread the word

    Do you part in spreading awareness to others through conversation and social media. Share news stories, educate others, and encourage their help in saving the planet.
